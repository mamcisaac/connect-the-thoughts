How to Play

Welcome to Connect-the-thoughts! Here's how you play:

  1. Look at the words at the top of each column and the beginning of each row.
  2. Your goal is to place the given clues in the correct cells. Each clue is individually connected to both the column and row words, but not necessarily in a way that connects the two words to each other.
  3. Drag and drop each clue into the cell where you think it fits best.
  4. Click "Check Answers" to see if you are correct. You have three attempts per puzzle.
  5. If you get stuck, click "Reset" to try again with the current words, or "New Game" for a completely new set of words and clues.
Regular Mode:
In this mode, the clues will change color based on their placement:
  • Green: The clue is in the correct cell.
  • Yellow: The clue is in the correct row or column.
  • Red: The clue is neither in the correct row nor the correct column.
Hard Mode:
In this mode, the colors of the columns and rows will change based on correctness when you check your answers:
  • Green: All answers in the row or column are correct.
  • Yellow: Some answers in the row or column are correct.
  • Red: No answers in the row or column are correct.

Example:
If the column word is "Apple" and the row word is "Tree", a fitting clue might be "Fruit". Place "Fruit" in the cell where the "Apple" column and "Tree" row intersect.
